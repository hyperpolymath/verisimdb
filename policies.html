Policies & Ethical Standards

Our commitment to ethical development, human rights, security, and accessibility.

Human Rights & Ethics

Modern Slavery Act Compliance

This project unequivocally opposes all forms of modern slavery, human trafficking, and forced labor. We:

  • Ensure all contributors work voluntarily and are fairly compensated
  • Do not tolerate exploitation in our supply chain
  • Report suspected slavery or trafficking to appropriate authorities
  • Comply with the UK Modern Slavery Act 2015 and equivalent legislation

Anti-Money Laundering (AML)

We maintain transparent financial practices and:

  • Accept no anonymous donations or contributions
  • Verify identities of significant financial contributors
  • Report suspicious transactions per regulatory requirements
  • Maintain public records of project funding sources

Data Sovereignty

We respect users' rights to control their data:

  • No data collection without explicit informed consent
  • Data processed in accordance with GDPR, CCPA, and local laws
  • Users may request data export, modification, or deletion
  • Transparent about data storage locations and processors
  • No sale of personal data to third parties

Accessibility Commitment

This site adheres to WCAG 2.3 Level AA (targeting AAA where possible):

  • Perceivable: Text alternatives, captions, adaptable layouts, distinguishable content
  • Operable: Keyboard accessible, sufficient time, seizure-safe, navigable
  • Understandable: Readable text, predictable operation, input assistance
  • Robust: Compatible with assistive technologies, future-proof markup

Report accessibility issues: Contact Us

Security Best Practices

  • HTTPS Only: All traffic encrypted with TLS 1.3+
  • Security Headers: HSTS, CSP, X-Frame-Options, etc.
  • No Tracking: Zero analytics, cookies, or fingerprinting
  • Subresource Integrity: All external resources verified
  • Responsible Disclosure: See security.txt

Open Source Commitment

Licensed under PMPL-1.0-or-later (Palimpsest License):

  • Freedom to use, modify, and distribute
  • Copyleft provisions ensure derivatives remain open
  • Ethical use requirements prohibit harmful applications
  • Attribution and transparency required

AI & Machine Learning Policy

Per our AI Bot Data Protocol manifest:

  • Scraping Allowed: AI bots may scrape for training with attribution
  • Training Allowed: Content may be used in ML training datasets
  • Attribution Required: Must credit original source and author
  • License Preserved: Derivatives must respect PMPL-1.0-or-later
  • Ethical Use Only: Prohibit use for surveillance, weapons, discrimination

Privacy Policy

Summary: We collect zero personal data.

  • No cookies (except essential session tokens if authenticated features added)
  • No analytics or tracking pixels
  • No fingerprinting or device identification
  • No third-party embeds (no social media widgets, ads, etc.)
  • No IP address logging beyond standard server access logs (30-day retention)

Contact Form: If you use our contact form, we temporarily store your message to respond. Data deleted after 90 days unless ongoing correspondence.

Content Policy

  • All content original or properly attributed
  • No plagiarism or copyright infringement
  • Respectful, inclusive language
  • No hate speech, harassment, or discrimination
  • Corrections published promptly when errors identified
